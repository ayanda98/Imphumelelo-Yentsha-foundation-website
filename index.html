Our Mission and Vision

Our Mission

To empower aspiring entrepreneurs and innovators in Eswatini by providing access to startup capital, mentorship, and essential digital skills, fostering sustainable economic growth and community development.

Our Vision

To make Eswatini a resilient and progressively self-sustaining nation by empowering 30% of youth and entrepreneurs with innovation and digital skills, establishing four regional innovation hubs, and increasing locally driven enterprises by 25%, through partnerships that promote inclusive and sustainable economic growth by 2031.
