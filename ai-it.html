Training the Next Digital Generation

Providing essential digital literacy, specialized IT skills, and an introduction to Artificial Intelligence to empower Eswatini's future workforce.

Our Core Training Pillars

Digital Literacy

Foundational skills including basic computer operations, internet safety, and effective use of office software and cloud tools.

Specialized IT Skills

Training in areas like web design (HTML/CSS), basic programming, and data management relevant to modern small businesses.

Introduction to AI

Understanding the basics of Artificial Intelligence and how to leverage AI tools for business efficiency and innovation.

Hands-On, Practical Learning

Our methodology ensures graduates are job-ready and capable of applying their knowledge immediately. We focus on project-based learning and real-world scenarios.

  • Practical Workshops: 70% of training time is dedicated to hands-on exercises and coding sessions.
  • Industry Expert Guest Lecturers: Learn directly from professionals working in the African tech sector.
  • Post-Course Mentoring: Continued support to help graduates find jobs or integrate IT into their new businesses.
