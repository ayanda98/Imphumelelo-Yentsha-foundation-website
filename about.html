The Genesis of Imphumelelo Yensha

Founded in 2023, Imphumelelo Yensha, meaning "New Success" in SiSwati, was created in direct response to the urgent need for youth empowerment and entrepreneurial growth in Eswatini.

We recognized a gap: local talent possessed immense potential, but lacked access to critical resourcesâ€”namely, startup capital and modern digital skill training. Our founders envisioned a bridge that would connect these aspiring leaders to the tools they need to succeed, fostering genuine, long-term economic independence.

Our Guiding Principles

Mission

To empower aspiring entrepreneurs and innovators in Eswatini by providing access to startup capital, mentorship, and essential digital skills, fostering sustainable economic growth and community development.

Vision

To make Eswatini a resilient and progressively self-sustaining nation by empowering 30% of youth and entrepreneurs with innovation and digital skills, increasing locally driven enterprises by 25% by 2031.
