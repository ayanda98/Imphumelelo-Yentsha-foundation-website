About Imphumelelo Yensha Foundation

We are dedicated to empowering individuals and building a prosperous Eswatini.

Why We Do This

At Imphumelelo Yensha Foundation, we believe every young person in Eswatini deserves a real opportunity to bring their dreams to life. While many funding programs and resources exist in our country, most young people struggle to access them because they lack the required contribution or support needed to qualify. Thatâ€™s where we step in. Imphumelelo Yensha Foundation helps bridge this gap by assisting youth with the required percentage needed to access revolving funds, while also providing mentorship, business guidance, and digital skills training. We do this because we believe that every dream is worth a try, and with the right bridge, the youth of Eswatini can build sustainable businesses and a brighter future for all

Our Mission

To empower aspiring entrepreneurs and innovators in Eswatini by providing access to startup capital, mentorship, and essential digital skills, fostering sustainable economic growth and community development.

Our Vision

A resilient and self-sustaining Eswatini where every individual has the opportunity and resources to turn their innovative ideas into reality, creating a prosperous future for all.

Our Values

  • Empowerment: We believe in the potential of every individual and strive to provide the tools and support needed for success.
  • Exellence: We strive to deliver the highest quality in everything we do, ensuring our programs, mentorship, and initiatives create real, lasting impact.
  • Innovation: We embrace creativity and forward-thinking solutions to address challenges and seize opportunities.
  • Sustainability: We focus on long-term impact, ensuring that our initiatives contribute to lasting economic and social development.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Imphumelelo Yensha Foundation?

Imphumelelo Yensha Foundation is a non-profit organization dedicated to empowering entrepreneurs and innovators in Eswatini by providing startup capital, mentorship, and training in AI & IT. Inspired by a Canadian Family (Perrots), whom their main aim was to help young people reach their full potential, but their sudden death left their work hanging, thus New Hope Foundation which aims to continue their good work in ESwatini.

How can I apply for a business loan?

You can apply for a business loan by visiting our Business Support page and filling out the application form. Our team will review your application and get in touch with you.

What kind of AI & IT training do you offer?

We offer training modules that cover basic computer skills, an introduction to Artificial Intelligence (including tools like ChatGPT), and cybersecurity basics. You can find more information on our AI & IT Training page.

Can I donate to a specific program?

Yes, on our Donate page, you can specify whether you would like your donation to go towards Business Development, AI & ICT Training, or general operations.

How can I contact the foundation?

You can reach us through the form on our Contact Us page, or by phone and email listed on that page.
