About New Hope Foundation

We are dedicated to empowering individuals and building a prosperous Eswatini.

Why We Do This

New Hope Foundation is a unique platform that allows every young person to dream, ESwatini is so rich when it comes to resources and support for young people, but then the youth lack the bridge to tape into those resources, for example to start a project you need funds or certain percentage to be granted those funds, Business Seminars are expensive to attend, mentorship and coaching is costly, laptops and IT lessons are costly as the world is moving digital, New Hope Foundation is creating the Bridge to that we bealive that every dream is worth a try giving New Hope to Everyone..

Our Mission

To empower aspiring entrepreneurs and innovators in Eswatini by providing access to startup capital, mentorship, and essential digital skills, fostering sustainable economic growth and community development.

Our Vision

A resilient and self-sustaining Eswatini where every individual has the opportunity and resources to turn their innovative ideas into reality, creating a prosperous future for all.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is New Hope Foundation?

New Hope Foundation is a non-profit organization dedicated to empowering entrepreneurs and innovators in Eswatini by providing startup capital, mentorship, and training in AI & IT. Inspired by a Canadian Family (Perrots), whom their main aim was to help young people reach their full potential, but their sudden death left their work hanging, thus the Imphumelelo Yentsha Foundation which aims to continue their good work in ESwatini.

How can I apply for a business loan?

You can apply for a business loan by visiting our Business Support page and filling out the application form. Our team will review your application and get in touch with you.

What kind of AI & IT training do you offer?

We offer training modules that cover basic computer skills, an introduction to Artificial Intelligence (including tools like ChatGPT), and cybersecurity basics. You can find more information on our AI & IT Training page.

Can I donate to a specific program?

Yes, on our Donate page, you can specify whether you would like your donation to go towards Business Development, AI & ICT Training, or general operations.

How can I contact the foundation?

You can reach us through the form on our Contact Us page, or by phone and email listed on that page.
